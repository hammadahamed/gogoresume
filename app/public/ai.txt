# About GoGoResume
GoGoResume is an AI-powered resume optimization tool that helps job seekers:
- Tailor resumes to match job descriptions automatically
- Optimize resumes for ATS (Applicant Tracking Systems)
- Autofill job application forms via Chrome extension
- Extract and match keywords from job descriptions
